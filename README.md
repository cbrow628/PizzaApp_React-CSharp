A personal dream of mine is to open up a pizza shop of my own one day.  I've been making homemade pizza's for years and love to share them with friends and family.  This app is just a way to dream a little.  I built it in React and C#/.NET.  It includes account creation with details and history, specialty and customized pizzas, and the start of a checkout process (its not a real business yet so no payments).
